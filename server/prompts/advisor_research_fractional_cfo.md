THIS CANDIDATE IS A FRACTIONAL OR OUTSOURCED CFO. Additional research focus.

SUBTYPE:
1. Solo independent fractional CFO — has 3–8 client engagements at any time.
2. Producer at a fractional CFO platform — CFO Hub, B2B CFO, Preferred CFO, Driven
   Insights, NOW CFO, vCFO, The CEO's Right Hand. These have varying compensation
   structures — confirm if the individual owns their book.
3. Junior partner at an outsourced accounting / CFO-services firm — Bench, Pilot, Acuity,
   Summit CPA, indinero, etc.

BACKGROUND CONTEXT — fractional CFOs almost always came from somewhere first:
- Former Controller or VP Finance at SMB(s).
- Former CFO at a private company.
- Came up through public accounting (Big 4 audit -> industry -> fractional).
- Investment banking background (sell-side or buy-side, lower-middle-market) — these are
  STRONG candidates because they know our world.

CREDENTIALS TO LOOK FOR:
- CPA (still relevant, but they're not in CPA practice).
- MBA from a strong program.
- CFA (less common, signals capital-markets sophistication).
- CMA (Certified Management Accountant) — common for fractional CFOs.
- CEPA / CExP — high signal for exit-prep specialization.

SPECIALTY SIGNALS — confirm:
- Practice serves trades / home services / construction / contractor businesses.
- Has done sale-prep work for clients (look for "raised capital" / "sold business" /
  "managed exit" language in case studies).
- Specializes in growth-stage SMBs ($5M–$50M revenue) — our zone.
- Works on cash-flow forecasting, KPI dashboards, board reporting, banking relationships
  — operational CFO work, not just bookkeeping.

HUNGER SIGNALS specific to fractional CFOs:
- Recently independent (last 0–5 years).
- Building client roster (sub-8 engagements).
- Active content on enterprise value, exit prep, cash flow.
- Publishes case studies of client wins.

RISK FLAGS:
- Some fractional CFOs run their own informal sell-side processes for clients — this is
  competitive with us. Confirm by looking at their service menu.
- Platform-employed fractional CFOs may have restrictions on outside referrals (less
  common than insurance but possible).
- Existing M&A referral partnerships with specific advisors / brokers.

OUTREACH ANGLE GENERATION — bias toward:
- "Graduating engagements" — when a client gets to exit-ready, hand off to us cleanly.
  Fractional CFO keeps the financial-modeling and Q-of-E support work on the sale,
  doesn't lose the relationship.
- Pre-sale clean-up engagements WE refer to THEM: owners we meet who have messy books
  and aren't sellable yet — they need a fractional CFO for 6–12 months first.
- Quality-of-Earnings prep work: we can refer them to lead the Q-of-E for our deals as
  the sell-side accountant.
- Joint content on "what your books need to look like to sell."
