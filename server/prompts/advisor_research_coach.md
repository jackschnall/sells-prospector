THIS CANDIDATE IS A BUSINESS COACH, PEER-GROUP LEADER, OR INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC CONSULTANT.
Additional research focus — this is the highest-signal segment, treat with care.

SUBTYPE CONFIRMATION (very different research paths):
- EOS Implementer — verify on eosworldwide.com/find-an-implementer. Note: years certified,
  Expert / Professional level, # of public testimonials, industries they highlight.
- Vistage Chair / YPO Chair / TEC Chair — verify on vistage.com or YPO directory. Note:
  group(s) they chair, years as chair, member tenure.
- Scaling Up coach (verityb.com directory) / Strategic Coach coach / Gravitas Impact / Petra coach.
- Trade-specific: Nexstar Network coaches (nexstarnetwork.com — limited public visibility,
  some by name), Service Roundtable / Service Nation coaches, ServiceTitan implementation
  consultants, Profit Rhino coaches, Service MVP, CertaPro / mr. handyman franchise consultants.
- ProVisors / BNI power-team leaders.
- Independent business coaches with their own brand.

ROSTER / SCALE INDICATORS — these matter more than firm size:
- # of active clients or members (often disclosed publicly).
- "Accepting new members" / "openings available" language in marketing.
- Roster turnover signals — recent testimonials vs. stale ones.
- Public client logos / case studies.

SPECIALTY SIGNALS — confirm:
- Coaches in trades / home services SPECIFICALLY (this is gold). Look for HVAC,
  plumbing, electrical, roofing, painting, landscaping, pest control, etc. in their
  case studies.
- Coaches on "scaling," "succession," "second-stage," or "exit-ready" themes.
- Has been a business owner themselves (especially a trades-business owner before
  becoming a coach — very high signal).

HUNGER SIGNALS specific to coaches:
- Newly certified (last 1–3 years).
- Building roster (sub-50 active clients).
- "Free strategy call" / lead magnet activity.
- Active in coach-network communities (mastermind groups, peer learning).
- Recently spun out their own coaching brand vs. operating under a larger umbrella.

RISK FLAGS:
- Some trade-specific consultancies have internal M&A or brokerage practices that
  compete (Nexstar in particular has been building M&A capabilities — confirm).
- Some peer-group networks have exclusivity rules on member referrals.
- EOS has its own preferred referral partners in some areas.

OUTREACH ANGLE GENERATION — bias toward:
- "Graduating clients" framing: members who've maxed out what the coach can teach them
  and are now ready to exit. The coach gets to keep the relationship intact while we
  handle the sale — they look like a hero, not someone who lost a client.
- "Bringing the exit conversation into the peer group": offer to do a short, free
  educational session for their members on exit readiness — pure value-add.
- Trade-specific deal data — coaches who specialize in HVAC will care a lot about
  HVAC roll-up multiples in their region. Share quietly.
- Refer business owners we meet who aren't ready to sell but need operational coaching.
