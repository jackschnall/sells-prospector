Market Intelligence
Top US metros ranked for plumbing M&A prospecting based on a weighted composite score.
Methodology
Each metro is scored 0–10 using a three-tier signal framework: Tier 1 (60%) — Market fundamentals: MSA population size, population growth rate, median home value, new housing permit volume, housing stock age, and plumbing company density. Tier 2 (25%) — Deal activity: estimated M&A activity in the residential trades space, PE platform presence, and owner age demographics. Tier 3 (15%) — Geographic fit: Southeast US and Sun Belt markets are weighted higher based on Sells’ existing deal flow and travel logistics. Population and housing data are sourced from U.S. Census Bureau estimates and building permit surveys. Plumbing density is derived from BLS QCEW establishment counts. M&A activity scores reflect observed platform and sponsor activity in each metro. Saturation tracks how many target-size companies ($5–50M revenue) have been identified versus the estimated addressable universe (~1 per 250K population).
